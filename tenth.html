Undergraduate Degree Programmes

The Department of Computer Science offers two undergraduate degree programs: the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology and the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1). In order to be eligible for admission to Level 200 Computer Science programs, applicants must have completed the following Level 100 courses: MATH 121: Trigonometry and Algebra AMAT 122: First Calculus Introduction to Computer Science I, CSCD 101 CSCD 102: Computer Science I Introduction II Each student in the computer science department is required to have a laptop that meets the following requirements, at the very least: i3 64-bit CPU; 8GB RAM; 500GB hard drive or 256 SSD
